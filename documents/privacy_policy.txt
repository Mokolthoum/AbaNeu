VULNBANK PRIVACY POLICY
=======================
Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
- Personal identification: Full name, date of birth, national ID
- Contact information: Email address, phone number, mailing address
- Financial data: Account numbers, transaction history, balance information
- Technical data: IP address, browser type, login timestamps

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
- To provide and maintain banking services
- To process transactions and send transaction confirmations
- To detect and prevent fraud
- To comply with legal obligations

3. DATA STORAGE
- All data is stored on secure servers located in the United States
- Database: SQLite (development) / PostgreSQL (production)
- Encryption: Data at rest uses AES-256 encryption
- Backups are performed daily and stored in /backup/ directory

4. DATA RETENTION
- Account data: Retained for 7 years after account closure
- Transaction records: Retained for 10 years
- Support tickets: Retained for 3 years

5. YOUR RIGHTS
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to request data deletion
- Right to data portability
